Notable Developers

Aldo Vargas

Thanks to Aldo's Tools for allowing inclusion of his work and apps! Aldo's Tools are essential for PS3 game management, including MultiMAN MOD, WebMAN MOD, and other homebrew apps he developed or contributed to.

DeanK

Also, thanks to DeanK for the original source of mmCM and original WebMAN! DeanK is renowned for multiMAN, a leading backup manager for the PS3.

Devil303

Devil303 has contributed various tools aimed at improving the PS3 homebrew experience, including important utility applications.

EvilNat

EvilNat has developed several custom firmware options and applications that significantly enhance the PS3 homebrew experience, making it easier for users to manage their systems and play homebrew games.

Bucanero

Bucanero is known for his contributions to custom firmware and various applications that expand the capabilities of the PS3, particularly in the realm of game management.

Joonie

Joonie has made significant contributions to the PS3 scene, including the development of various homebrew applications that improve system functionality and user experience.
