PS3 Firmware Reversion

Revert to Previous Firmware

Follow the steps below to revert your PS3 to a previous firmware version:

WARNING: This process requires a jailbroken PS3 and proper tools. Proceed with caution.
    Place the previous firmware in the root directory of a USB drive. Insert the USB drive into your PS3 and click I Understand - Proceed Follow the on-screen instructions to revert the firmware.
